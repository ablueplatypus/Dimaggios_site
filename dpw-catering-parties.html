DiMaggios Courtyard Room Makes Every Guest Feel Special.

On Site: Meetings, parties and other get-togethers take on a special ambiance in DiMaggios Courtyard Room. This newly renovated  room replicates a courtyard (cortile) you would find in Angelo’s hometown of Conversano in Bari, Italy. The Courtyard Room accommodates 20 to 50 guests.

Your guests will feel they are enjoying a special get together with good friends - in Italy. We invite you to review our Party Menus and discuss your guests special tastes and preferences with us. We will design a menu to meet your requirements.

Off Site: Catering is a pleasure for you and your guests when DiMaggio's provides the meal. You will have more time to spend with your guests while they enjoy your selections.

Either way, you and your guests will be pleased with the authentic tastes, freshness and quality of your selections.
