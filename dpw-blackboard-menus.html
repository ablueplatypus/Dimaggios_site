Service Announcement

We want to thank all of our loyal customers for their support.
In an effort to accommodate the increased demand for takeout and curbside, and allow us to better serve you,
we are asking if you can either place your orders in advance or space your orders between the hours of

12 noon and 7:30 PM

Most dishes can easily be re-heated in the comfort of your own home.

Please note, during peak times between 5:30 pm to 7:00 pm,the wait time for pickup is likely to be 1 hour depending on the volume of orders.

Thank you for your kindness and patience - we are committed to getting through this safely together!
