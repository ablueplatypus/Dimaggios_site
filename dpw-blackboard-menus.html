Service Announcement

We want to thank all of our loyal customers for their support. In an effort to keep within the social distance guidelines and to accommodate the increased demand for takeout and curbside with limited staff, we are asking if you can space your takeout orders between the hours of 4PM and 7PM or earlier.

Please note, during peak times between 5 PM to 7 PM, the wait time for pickup is likely to be 1 hour depending on the volume of orders being placed. Please place your orders in advance to allow us to better serve you. Order for dinner pickup can be placed any time throughout the day between 12 Noon and 7 PM. Most dishes can easily be reheated at your convenience in the comfort of your own kitchens. Thank you for your continued patronage.

In addition we are asking everyone to be more simplified and concise in their orders because we are working with a more limited menu. As soon as specials are avaliable again we will post them on our blackboard.

Thank you Eddy and Angelo
