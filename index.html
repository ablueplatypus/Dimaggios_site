Welcome to DiMaggio’s Trattoria & Bar

Since 1978, visitors to DiMaggio’s have been greeted with warm welcomes and traditional Old World fare in a casual atmosphere.

Complimenting the quality and ambiance of DiMaggios is the sweeping, hand painted mural, the stone pizza oven and the ever-changing blackboard with its daily specialty offerings.

However, the real attraction, and the reason DiMaggios has a loyal following, is the commitment to preparing food with only the finest and freshest ingredients. Each dish is made to order and we are happy to create a dish to your liking – just let us know.

And, if you are having a party, family event or just a large group of friends, be certain to ask about our private Courtyard Room.

Service Announcement

We want to thank all of our loyal customers for their support. In an effort to keep within the social distance guidelines and to accommodate the increased demand for takeout and curbside with limited staff, we are asking if you can space your takeout orders between the hours of 4PM and 7PM or erlier.

Please note, during peak times between 5PM to 7PM the wait time for pickup is likely to be 1 hour depending on the volume of orders being placed. Please place your orders in advance to allow us to better serve you. Order for dinner pickup can be placed any time throughout the day between 12 Noon and 7PM. Thank you for your continued patronage.

In addition we are asking everyone to be more simplified and concise in their orders becuase we are working with a more limited menu. As soon as specials are avaliable again we will post them on our blackboard.

Thank you Eddy and Angelo
