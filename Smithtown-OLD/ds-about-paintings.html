About Our Paintings

The paintings hanging in the DiMaggio's dining rooms and cocktail lounge are painted by Theresa Giannuzzi, a well known, accomplished artist and are rotated with the seasons. Theresa has studied under Charles Pasqualina and Howard Rose and has been painting for over 20 years. She specializes in Landscapes, Florals, Portraits, City Scapes and Pets. She is currently selling at Chrysalis Gallery in Southampton, NY and Les Bon Amis in Locust Valley, NY.

Her work is also on display at DiMaggio’s Port Washington location. Theresa is Angelo’s wife of some 30 years.

The mural along the full wall of DiMaggio’s Port Wasington dining room illustrates the Old World conviviality, charm and quality fare of Bari, Italy. The mural reminds us daily of the tradition and goals of the restaurant.
