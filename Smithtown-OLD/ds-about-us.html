Since 1978, visitors to DiMaggio’s have been greeted with warm welcomes and traditional Old World fare  in a casual atmosphere.

The owners of DiMaggio’s, Port Washington, have teamed up with Sal Formica to bring the DiMaggio dining and hospitality experience to Smithtown. Sal is a long time resident of the community, a Vice President of the Commack South Little League and a former Chief of the Commack Fire Department. Sal is also a retired NYC Detective.

Sal, Eddy and Angelo are committed to continuing the DiMaggio tradition of preparing each menu item with only the finest and freshest ingredients. Each dish is made to order and the staff will be happy to adjust or create the dish of your choice.

At DiMaggio’s there is always an owner on site. Here you might also get acquainted with Sal’s sons, Nicholas and Thomas who serve as runners and waiters. 

Complementing  the quality and ambiance of DiMaggios are the original paintings by Angelo’s wife, Theresa, and the ever-changing blackboard with its daily specialty offerings.

You will enjoy the casual ambiance of the dining room... And, don’t forget to stop by our upbeat cocktail lounge, designed for comfort and casual conversation.

And, if you are having a party, family event or just a large group of friends, be certain to ask about our private party availabilities. We can accommodate up to 70 people.
