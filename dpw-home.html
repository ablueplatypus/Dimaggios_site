Welcome to Port Washington!

Home of the Original DiMaggio’s Trattoria & Bar

We would like to announce that DiMaggio's Trattoria of Port Washington has recently divested itself of their Smithtown location. - Angelo and Eddie

Since 1978, visitors to DiMaggio’s have been greeted with warm welcomes and traditional Old World fare in a casual atmosphere.

Complimenting the quality and ambiance of DiMaggios is the sweeping, hand painted mural, the stone pizza oven and the ever-changing blackboard with its daily specialty offerings.

However, the real attraction, and the reason DiMaggios has a loyal following, is the commitment to preparing food with only the finest and freshest ingredients. Each dish is made to order and we are happy to create a dish to your liking – just let us know.

And, if you are having a party, family event or just a large group of friends, be certain to ask about our private Courtyard Room.
