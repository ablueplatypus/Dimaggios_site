About Our Mural

The mural along the full wall of DiMaggio’s dining room illustrates the Old World conviviality, charm and quality fare of Bari, Italy. The mural reminds us daily of the tradition and goals of the restaurant.

The mural was painted by Theresa Giannuzzi, a well known, accomplished artist. For over 20 years. She has studied under Charles Pasqualina and Howard Rose. She specializes in Landscapes, Florals, Portraits, City Scapes and Pets. She is currently selling at Chrysalis Gallery in Southampton, NY and Les Bon Amis in Locust Valley, NY.

More samples of Theresa’s artistry coming soon... plus more is available at her website www.artbytg.com
